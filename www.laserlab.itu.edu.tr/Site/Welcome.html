Laser Research Group

The Laser Research and Spectroscopy group of Istanbul Technical University Department of Physics Engineering conducts research on the optical properties of solids and it was established at 1994. Our group is an experimental group doing basic researches in the field of photonics under the leadership of Prof. Gönül Özen Eryürek. We have seven active members working in the group. Most of our investigations are in large band gap solids doped with transition metals and/or rare earth ions which act as an optically active centers.
